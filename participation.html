Participation

The First Steps of a Dixie National

✠ XII ✠

Step 1 — Self-Assessment

Before anything else, ask yourself:

  • Do you have at least 200 followers on an X account?
  • Do you have an anonymous email unconnected to your real name?
  • Do you have a Discord account set up for strictly anonymous use?

If you can answer yes to all three, proceed directly to Step 3. If not, continue to Step 2.

Step 2 — Prepare Your Persona

2.1 Create your Tools

  • Make a new, anonymous email.
  • Make a new X account reflecting the Southern, Christian, and aristocratic spirit.
    • Profile picture, banner, or bio should embody Dixie.
    • Anime profile pictures are permitted if properly adapted to Dixie symbolism.
  • Create a new Discord account for strictly anonymous use.

2.2 Gain 200 Followers

This is your first test:

  • Earn 200 followers by producing Dixie-themed content.
  • Focus on reply-posting to influential accounts.
  • Blend in naturally. The mission comes first, not clout.

Step 3 — Prepare Yourself

  • Study the Doctrine of Dixieland and the FAQ carefully.
  • Understand that unity of mind, speech, and spirit is expected.
  • Compose a 250-word essay on why you believe in Dixie First's vision.
    • Do not use AI.
    • Speak plainly and from the heart.

Step 4 — Submit Your Application

When ready, prepare and submit the following:

  • The Dixie state you claim.
  • Are you Christian? (Specify denomination.)
  • Your X and Discord handle.
  • Confirmation that you have read the Doctrine and FAQ.
  • Your 250-word essay from Step 3.

Step 5 — Make Contact

DM our official X account with a hand and timestamp:

  • A picture of your hand facing down.
  • A handwritten note showing:
    • Date and time.
    • The words: Hail Dixie.
    • A cross.

Further instructions will follow privately.
