Participation

The Path into the Tribe

✠ XII ✠

Step I: Forge Your Persona

Every tribesman must begin by crafting a Persona — a fully anonymous online identity. This will be your shield and your banner.

  • Email: Create a new anonymous email. Do not connect it to any personal accounts. Store the password offline.
  • Twitter (X): Create an account with a fitting Southern, Christian, or aristocratic identity. Use appropriate imagery for profile and banner. You can use an anime girl so long as a Dixie-related symbol is overlayed.
  • Discord: Create a new account for Tribe use. Avoid voice and video usage. This account is necessary for screening. Do not join other political servers with this account.
  • Optional Platforms: Consider creating Instagram, TikTok, or Telegram accounts for future use.

Step II: Prepare Yourself

  • Read: Fully internalize the Doctrine of Dixieland and go over the FAQ.
  • Mindset: This is duty, not hobby.

Step III: Craft Your Kit

Each man must prepare the following before entering the Gate:

  • 5 Memes: Original, fitting the tribe’s spirit. Southern identity. Christian. Aristocratic. Anti-modern. Bold and witty.
  • 250 Word Statement: Write your personal confession. Why do you stand with Dixieland? Speak plainly. AI writing is forbidden.

Step IV: Enter the Gate

  • Join: Enter the Tribe’s private Discord server through the proper channels.
  • Submit: Post your Meme Kit, Personal Statement, and a verification photo:
    • Photo of your hand with a handwritten note.
    • Include: Your alias, the time and date, a cross, and the words “Hail Dixie”.
  • Await Judgment: The existing Cells will quietly review your submission. You will receive acceptance, feedback, or dismissal.

Step V: Assignment and First Orders

  • Placement: You will be placed in an existing Cell or held in reserve until a new Cell forms.
  • Role Assignment: You will be given a role fitting your strengths:
    • Leader
    • Memesmith
    • Video Specialist
    • Reply Hound
    • Scout
  • Begin: Once assigned, you are no longer a candidate but a full member. Your Cell will give you your first tasks.

There is no fanfare, no ceremony, no applause. There is only the labor. Begin.
