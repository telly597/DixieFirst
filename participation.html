Participation

Entering the Covenant

Prepare

Participation in Dixieland is not membership in a club, but entrance into a sacred covenant. It is first spiritual, then social, then political. All who join must first know and accept the Doctrine of Dixieland without reservation.

Steps to Enter

  1. Read and affirm the Doctrine of Dixieland.
  2. Publicly or privately take the Tribal Pledge of Dixieland.
  3. Declare your household, kin, or solitary standing.
  4. Contact an elder or appointed registrar for inclusion in the living roll of the tribe.

Contact for Registration

To be recognized by the elders and inscribed into the rolls, you must present yourself, by word or letter, to one of the appointed men.

In due time, an official registry will be maintained under the authority of the council.

Gather. Prepare. Remember.
