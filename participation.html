Participation

The Path of Entry and Formation

✠ XII ✠

1. Create Your Mask

The first task of every tribesman is to create a War Mask — your anonymous digital identity. This identity is the face you will wear in all online spaces where the tribe operates. It is not simply a random alias; it is part of your craft. Without it, you have no shield, no banner, and no presence.

A well-crafted War Mask protects you, lends you authority, and allows you to move freely across the digital terrain.

Step-by-Step Instructions:

1.1. Create a New Anonymous Email

  • Do not connect this email to any of your personal accounts.
  • Do not use personal information, past usernames, or anything that can be traced to you.
  • Write down the password securely offline.

1.2. Create an X (Twitter) Account

This is your primary battleground.

Choose a name and handle that reflect the tribe’s spirit:

  • Aim for something that feels like a Christian, Southern, or aristocratic identity.
  • Example: @SouthernPillar, @RedRiverSquire, @CumberlandSon

Profile Picture:

  • Use the tribe’s style: Dixie flag, a tribal shield, Christian symbol, historic photo, or meaningful symbol. Yes, it can be an anime girl so long as it's a Dixie-related edit.

Bio:

  • Be simple, dignified, and subtle.
  • Speak as a Southern Christian returning to the old ways.

1.3. Create a Discord Account

  • Required for initial screening and internal tribal use.
  • Same as above: anonymous email.
  • Avoid using microphones or cameras at all times.
  • Do not join public political servers immediately with this account. Use only private links from trusted kin.

1.4. Optional Secondary Platforms

These are useful but not mandatory.

  • Telegram: Useful for content sharing, group chats, and private channels.
  • Instagram: For those who can handle visual and aesthetic content well.
  • TikTok: For the bold who can engage in short-form video creation with skill and discretion.

2. Strengthen Your Foundation

2.1. Read the Doctrine

Study the Dixieland Doctrine carefully. Absorb it. Understand the faith, the covenant, and the purpose.

2.2. Memorize the Motto

Deo Vindice (God our Vindicator)

This is not a slogan. It is a confession.

2.3. Set Personal Rules

  • No personal posts. No modern irony. No excuses.
  • Your War Mask exists to serve the tribe, not yourself.

2.4. Prepare Mentally

This work is not a hobby. It is duty. Take it as seriously as you would a real-world calling.

3. Screening Phase: Craft Basic Content Kits

3.1. Five Original Dixie Memes

  • Create at least 5 memes aligned with the tribe's spirit.
  • Southern identity
  • Christian faith
  • Aristocratic spirit
  • Anti-modernity
  • Humor

3.2. Personal Confession (250 Words)

Write 250 words on why you believe in Dixieland.

  • Do not use AI.
  • Grammar does not matter. Honesty does.
  • Speak as a son of the South who remembers.

4. Screening and Acceptance

4.1. Enter the Gate

Join the private Discord, where screening is quietly and orderly conducted.

4.2. Present Your Kit

  • Five Memes
  • Personal Statement
  • Proof of Kinship and Faith:
    • Submit a photo of your hand and a handwritten note with:
    • Your anonymous name.
    • The date and time.
    • The words: Hail Dixie.

4.3. Await Brotherly Judgment

Your screening will be reviewed by existing Cells.

You will receive either acceptance, asked to revise certain things, or quiet dismissal.

5. Assignment and Integration

5.1. Assignment to a Cell

Upon acceptance, you will be placed into an existing Cell or used to form a new one.

Each Cell is six men strong. If none are open, you will be held in reserve until enough men are gathered to form a fresh Cell.

No Cell is randomly assembled. Cells are built for strength, balance, and trust.

5.2. Role Selection

Each Cell carries out its work through defined roles. You will be assigned one of the following based on your strengths, skills, and initiative.

  • Leader — Guides the Cell, gives direction, and keeps order.
  • Memesmith — Creates graphics, memes, and propaganda.
  • Video Specialist — Crafts videos suited for the platforms of battle.
  • Reply Hound — Operates in replies, comment sections, and threads, guiding attention and shaping discussion.
  • Scout — Quietly seeks out new talent, identifies opportunities, and monitors trends.

You may hold more than one function if needed. Your labor will be plain to all, and roles may change as trust grows.

5.3. Begin the Work

Once your role is set and your Cell formed, you will be given basic tasks and objectives.

Cells operate independently but in harmony with the broader Tribe.

Your task is simple: carry the spirit of Dixieland into the field, speak the language of your people, and lay stones for what is to come.

You are now a brother among brothers. Take up your work.

DEO VINDICE
