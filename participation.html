The Entry Process into the Tribe

✠ XII ✠

1. Create Your War Mask

Every man begins by preparing his anonymous identity. This is not optional. It is the first discipline. Without it, you endanger yourself and your kin.

  • Create an anonymous email (ProtonMail, Tutanota, or another secure provider).
  • Create fresh accounts on X (Twitter), Discord, and optionally Telegram, Instagram, and TikTok.
  • Use no traceable information. Never link these accounts to your real phone number, personal email, or old accounts.

2. Prepare Your Household

Before entering the Tribe, a man must have his personal affairs in order. This is not merely about security, but about honor.

  • Secure all devices. Remove spyware. Learn the basics of operational security (OPSEC).
  • Prepare your mind. Study the Dixieland Doctrine until you know it by heart.
  • Prepare your spirit. Re-establish Christian discipline in your life: prayer, worship, and order.

3. Craft Basic Content Kits

Every man entering the Tribe contributes immediately by creating:

  • At least five original Dixie-themed memes.
  • A 250-word personal statement explaining your belief in Dixieland. Be sincere. Do not use AI to generate this.

4. Screening and Acceptance

Screening is done quietly within the private Discord server:

  • Submit your meme kit, personal statement, and a photo of your hand with a hand-drawn cross and timestamp.
  • Await review by the existing Cells. Acceptance is based on sincerity, competence, and discretion.

5. Assignment to a Cell

Once accepted, you will be placed into a Cell of six men. This will be your operational kin. Within the Cell you will receive a role according to your strengths:

  • Leader
  • Memesmith
  • Video Specialist
  • Reply Hound
  • Scout

From there, your true work begins.
