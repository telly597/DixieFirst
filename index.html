The Tribal Confederacy of Dixieland

A Tribe of the Twelve Nations Confederacy

"We are not a party. We are not a club. We are a people."

Before you is the hall of the Tribe of Dixieland, first among the Twelve Nations of America. Here, the covenant lives, the people remember, and the work of restoration begins. This is not an idea. This is our blood, our land, and our faith bound under Heaven.

The Covenant

Dixieland is gathered by faith, bound by blood, and ordered by the wisdom of our ancestors. Our duty is to preserve what was handed down to us, to restore what has been broken, and to raise up again the high things that have fallen. In this, we stand firm, guided by the cross and the memory of our forefathers.

To every son and daughter of the South, the door stands open. The covenant calls you.
